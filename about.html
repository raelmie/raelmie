Tili Ready Chess Club was founded in 2024 with the goal of bringing together individuals who enjoy chess in a competitive yet relaxed environment.

The club was built through the efforts of its founders and the valuable contributions of Wiseman, Sydney, and Ezekiel, whose involvement helped shape the structure and growth of the group.

Since its establishment, the club has successfully hosted several tournaments, with over 500 matches played among members. These competitions have fostered both skill development and strong rivalries within the group.

The vision of the club is to continue expanding its reach, creating opportunities for more players to join, compete, and enjoy chess at all levels — whether for serious competition or simply for fun.
