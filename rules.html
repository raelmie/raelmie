♟️ Tournament Rules & Regulations

ALL MATCHES MUST BE PLAYED ON CHESS.COM. No exceptions. No excuses. No creative alternatives.

1. Tournament Format

  • Round-robin format: each player faces every other player.
  • Each pairing consists of two games (one White, one Black).
  • Number of rounds determined by the organizer.
  • Top 4 advance to knockout Champions Round; others become highly respected spectators.

2. Match Structure

  • Each match consists of two games.
  • Players alternate colors.
  • Both games must be completed for results to count.

3. Scoring System

  • Win both games (2–0): 3 Points | GP: 2
  • Win 1, draw 1: 3 Points | GP: 1.5
  • Win 1, lose 1: 1 Point | GP: 1
  • Draw both: 1 Point | GP: 1
  • Lose 1, draw 1: 0 Points | GP: 0.5
  • Lose both (0–2): 0 Points | GP: 0

Game Points (GP) are used as a tie-breaker.

4. Tournament Standings

  • Total Points (primary)
  • Game Points (secondary)
  • Further tie-breaks decided by organizer if needed

5. Match Scheduling

  • Maximum of two matches per player per day.
  • Schedule optimized to minimize total duration.
  • Failure to appear within 45 minutes = automatic forfeit.
  • Both players absent = automatic draw. No debates.

6. Time Control

  • Set before tournament begins.
  • Players must stay within time limits.
  • Timeout = loss. Chess is not a meditation retreat.

7. Conduct of Players

  • Sportsmanship required.
  • Friendly trash talk allowed. Foul language is not.
  • No cheating or external assistance.
  • VoIP allowed but optional.
  • Hinting is prohibited and punishable.

8. Recording of Results

  • Players must ensure results are recorded correctly.
  • Submitted results are final unless disputed immediately.

9. Disputes

  • Report immediately to the organizer.
  • Organizer’s decision is final.

10. Amendments

  • Organizer may amend rules if necessary.
  • Changes will be communicated beforehand.

11. Acceptance of Rules

  • Participation = full agreement to these rules.
